Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo, born on February 5th 1985, a Portuguese footballer who plays as a winger or striker for Spanish club Real Madrid and the captain of the Portuguese national team.

Ronaldo began his career as a youth player for Andorinha, where he played for two years, before moving to Nacional. He was the first player to win all four main PFA and FWA awards. In addition, he won the European Golden Shoe award two times, becoming the first player to win the trophy in two different championships.
